OUR STORY

We met on the first day of optometry school at Western University of Health Sciences. During an orientation week ice breaker, we were asked to stand up when different activities that interested us were called out. Jackie noticed that Michael (who was sitting in front of her and hadn’t noticed her yet) was standing up A LOT. Rather than feeling impressed, she was intrigued by how arrogant he seemed.

At the next break, Jackie noticed Michael talking to some people (probably bragging about himself some more) and went over to see what he was about. She yelled, "You’re that guy who does everything!" As soon as Michael saw her, he was smitten. "Wow, look at this pretty girl so impressed by all of my activities!" Fortunately, we became fast friends and spent the rest of the day together, talking, laughing, and getting to know each other.

A few days later, Michael asked Jackie out. Jackie said no, explaining that she wanted to focus on school. However, rather than being awkward, we remained close friends, studying and hanging out together as usual. A few weeks after that, Jackie realized Michael was an "angel from the heavens" and not arrogant after all. We began dating and have been happy in love ever since.
